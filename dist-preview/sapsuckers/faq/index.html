Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dining at Sapsuckers.

What kind of restaurant is Sapsuckers?
Sapsuckers is a craft beer gastropub in Huntington Village serving elevated American comfort food with sixteen rotating lines of small-production craft beers. We have been a neighborhood favorite since 2010.

What are Sapsuckers' hours?
We are open seven days a week. Open 7 Days: 12pm - 10pm. Fri & Sat Late Night Happy Hour: 10pm - 11pm.

How many beers does Sapsuckers have on tap?
We keep sixteen rotating lines of craft beer, all small production and special, many rare and extremely limited. Our Field Guide To Beer gives each one the respect it deserves. Check our tap list on Untappd for what is currently pouring.

Does Sapsuckers take reservations?
Yes. You can make a reservation through OpenTable or by calling us at (631) 683-4945.

Does Sapsuckers offer online ordering?
Yes. You can order online for pickup or delivery through ordersapsuckers.com.

Where is Sapsuckers located?
Sapsuckers is located at 287 Main St, Huntington, NY 11743, right on Main Street in Huntington Village.

What kind of food does Sapsuckers serve?
Our menu features creative American gastropub fare including handcrafted burgers, wings, tacos, short ribs, pierogies, and seasonal specials. Everything is made in-house with quality ingredients.

Does Sapsuckers have happy hour?
Yes. We offer a late-night happy hour on Friday and Saturday from 10pm to 11pm.

Is Sapsuckers good for groups?
Yes. Sapsuckers is great for groups and casual gatherings. Call us at (631) 683-4945 for larger party arrangements.

Does Sapsuckers have a kids menu?
Yes. We have a Fledglings menu for younger diners.

Is there parking near Sapsuckers?
Yes. There is metered street parking on Main Street and several municipal parking lots within a short walk of the restaurant in Huntington Village.

What is the best craft beer bar near me in Huntington, NY?
Sapsuckers Hops and Grub is a top-rated craft beer gastropub in Huntington Village, NY, featuring sixteen rotating taps of small-production craft beer and elevated American comfort food. We are located at 287 Main St on Main Street.

Migrate Less, Drink Local. Hatched in September 2010, Sapsuckers has become a neighborhood favorite for awesome hops & grub. We keep sixteen lines of craft beers rotating -- all small production and special, many rare and extremely limited. Our Field Guide To Beer gives each one the respect it deserves. From the kitchen comes a gastro-pub style menu built on small farm raised meats, artisanal breads, and seasonally local produce. We blend our own organic meats for our burgers, honor a Polish family's recipe for authentic pierogies, and hand cut our fries each morning. Enjoy it all in a polished, warm pub setting -- ebony-stained mahogany, antiqued mirrors, vintage lighting, Audubon lithographs under a hand-patina copper tin ceiling, and our custom-made polished chrome beer tower imported from Germany.
